Conquered Frontiers of Physics

- a tribute to historical descriptions of our reality -

Humanity is the only species known to communicate ideas through written symbols; an act of immortal potential conveying a fragment of the writer's mind to his fellow descendants. This is the secret we use to conquer an understanding of ever-expanding frontiers of modern maths, physics, chemistry, geology, and biology. This page is a tribute to a few historic moments that lead to our ability to grasp at the heavens.
